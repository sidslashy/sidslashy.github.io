SWORD BREAK

This game was created as a part of Movie Game Jam 2018, it was my first game jam, and my premier project in the Godot Game engine. We had a team of 3, people from across the globe. Our team consisted of a brilliant artist (who came up with the concept and design) and a sound engineer and me who played the role of the programmer.
The theme of the jam was to recreate one of our favorite movie scenes and we recreated the giant skeleton scene from the movie Kubo and the two strings.
This game was created in 10 days and was ranked #4 in terms of Game Play and Innovations and an overall rank of #12 out of 116 entries.

LUCKY NORTH CASINO

Lucky North Casino is a social casino game developed by Ruby Seven Studios. This was one of the first games I've worked on professionally.
